CIVIC TYPE R. IS IT WORTH IT?

By Jeffrey Hermo

The Civic Type R has been an icon especially to Honda Civic fanatics. A little history about the "Type R" badge is it was based on the Generation 6 of the Honda Civic namely Civic "EK". The engine of the first Type R is a B16B engine. It is a naturally aspirated engine that 182 horsepower at 7500 RPM of torque. The interior also contains a bucket Recaro seats and a Type R red floor mats. A similar of this was released in Asia which is named Honda Civic SIR which has the same engine but can be also a daily driven car.

Now, the Civic Type R has come a long way from its predecessor. In fact, it once held a Nurbrugring record of 7-minute, 43.8 second lap beating the record of Volkswagen Golf GTI Clubsport S. It is also important to note that the Civic Type R is a front wheel drive.

Dubbed as the ultimate and must have of the civic enthusiast, it is clearly priced above your normal daily drives. At the Philippines, it is priced as 3.6 million or 89000 cad due to import taxes being impose on cars considered as luxury while here in Canada, it is priced hald around 36,000 to 44,000 cad. Still, with that price civic enthusiast will still buy one as for them it is already a way of life.

PERFORMANCE AND ENGINE

This civic is powered by a 2.0 liter turbocharged engine with a manual transmission. This civic weighs just over 3100 pounds and have a very low center of gravity. It is designed to be very aerodynamic and body designs are specifically made to make the wind flow and not go against it. It is very comfortable on ride even though it is equipped with a 20 inch wheel.

SAFETY FEATURES

  • Standard forward-collision warning and automated emergency braking
  • Standard lane-departure warning and lane-keeping assist
  • Standard adaptive cruise control
  • Brembo big brakes

INTERIOR FEATURES

Honda's latest infotainment system should run through a The standard 7.0-inch touchscreen allows gesture controls similar to a tablet or smartphone. A 12-speaker, 540-watt sound system is standard, along with SiriusXM satellite radio. While the Type R has many standard features, an auxiliary input and a Wi-Fi hotspot are not available. The Type R also has a flat-bottom steering wheel, faux-suede trim and a solid aluminum shift knob.The 7.0-inch digital gauge cluster has a red theme and a distinct appearance for each drive mode. Its features include navigation, a G-force meter, and a lap timer.

Clearly, this car is ready for your daily driving or the racetrack. It can satisfy your need for speed or your just clean and fun driving.
